üé≤ Gameplay Flow

Opening Play: The starting player MUST include their qualifying card (3‚ô¶ or lowest card) in their first combination
Following Plays: Next player (anti-clockwise) must play the same type of combination but stronger, or pass
Round Continues: Players keep playing or passing until all players pass consecutively
New Round: Last player to play a combination starts the next round with any valid combination
Game End: When one player discards all cards, the round ends immediately

üí° Key Strategic Points

  • Opening Hand: The starting player MUST include their qualifying card (3‚ô¶ or lowest card if 3‚ô¶ is not in play) in their first combination
  • Combination Lock: Once a combination type is played (e.g., pair), all following plays that round must be the same type
  • Passing Strategy: You can pass even if you have playable cards - sometimes it's strategic to save strong cards
  • Control: Winning a round gives you control to start the next round with any combination type
  • Card Management: Balance between playing cards to reduce your hand and keeping strong combinations for control
  • Endgame: Watch opponents' card counts - sometimes it's better to prevent someone from going out than to reduce your own cards

üéØ Victory Conditions

  • Round Victory: First player to discard all 13 cards
  • Game Victory: Player with the lowest score when someone reaches 50 points
  • Perfect Round: Going out while opponents still have many cards maximizes their penalties
